Winhouse

Winhouse is the product of the creative connection and friendship between Susie Godwin and Lisa Lofthouse. They wanted to find a way to express their creativity using recycled and reclaimed building materials and household items.

The pair began by making pieces that celebrate the reemergence of Downtown Fayetteville as the center of our community. By taking photos of the architecture downtown and combining them with used pieces of wood and found objects, a unique art form was created.
